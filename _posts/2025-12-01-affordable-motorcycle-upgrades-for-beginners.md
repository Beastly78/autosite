Before we dive into the specifics, it's essential to note that performance upgrades should be done carefully to avoid compromising your bike's safety and warranty. Always consult your owner's manual or speak with a professional mechanic before making any changes.

### Upgrade 1: Intake and Exhaust Upgrades
#### Cost: $100-$500
#### Pros:
* Improved engine performance and horsepower
* Increased fuel efficiency
* Enhanced sound and exhaust note
#### Cons:
* May require modifications to engine and exhaust system
* Can be noisy
* Warranty voidance possible

### Upgrade 2: Suspension Upgrades
#### Cost: $200-$1,000
#### Pros:

#### Cons:
* Can be expensive
* Requires professional installation
* May void warranty

### Upgrade 3: Brake Upgrades
#### Cost: $100-$500
#### Pros:
* Improved stopping power and control
* Enhanced safety

#### Cons:

* Can be noisy
