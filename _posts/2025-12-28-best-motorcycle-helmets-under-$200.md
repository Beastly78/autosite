When it comes to motorcycles, safety is paramount. One of the most critical components of a rider's gear is their helmet. Not only does it protect their head in the event of an accident, but it also provides a secure and comfortable fit. **Section 1: Why Safety Matters**
-----------------------------

Before we dive into the best motorcycle helmets under $200, it's essential to understand why safety is crucial for riders. According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA), a helmet can reduce the risk of fatal head injury by 37% and the risk of serious head injury by 69%. With so many dangers on the road, investing in a high-quality helmet is essential for any motorcyclist.

**Section 2: Types of Motorcycle Helmets**
--------------------------------------

There are several types of motorcycle helmets available, each with its unique features and benefits. When it comes to budget-friendly options, here are some popular choices:

* **Full-Face Helmets**: Provide comprehensive protection for the face, head, and neck.
* **Half-Helmets**: Offer protection for the top of the head, but leave the face and neck vulnerable.
* **Open-Face Helmets**: Provide protection for the top of the head, but do not cover the face.
* **Off-Road Helmets**: Designed for off-road riding, these helmets typically have a more aggressive design and additional features like chin guards.

**Section 4: Factors to Consider When Choosing a Helmet**
------------------------------------------------------

When choosing the best motorcycle helmet under $200, there are several factors to consider:

* **Weight:** A lighter helmet can reduce fatigue and discomfort during long rides.
* **Fit:** Ensure the helmet fits comfortably and securely on your head.
* **Visor:** Consider a helmet with an integrated anti-fog visor or the ability to install one.
* **Certification:** Ensure the helmet is certified by a reputable organization, such as the DOT or SNELL.
* **Durability:** Look for high-quality construction and materials to ensure the helmet lasts.
